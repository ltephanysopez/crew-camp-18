About CREW

Career Readiness and Empowerment of Women (CREW) is a multidisciplinary year-long internship program for young women in Mission, TX. Interns in this program will develop professional skills through workshops, assignments, and group discussion. Along with professional development, CREW interns will be exposed to a diverse range of career opportunities by hearing from guest speakers in the STEAM and entrepreneurship fields. Interns will work on a project that will solve a problem in their community, and then complete an intensive web development course to create a website for their project from scratch.
